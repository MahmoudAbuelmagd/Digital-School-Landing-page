Available Workshops Include

3D Game Development

Students design and build interactive 3D games while learning core game mechanics, logic, and digital creativity.

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

A beginner-friendly exploration of AI concepts, applications, and real-world use cases — helping students understand how AI works and how it shapes the world around them.

Building Web Apps (No-Code)

Students create functional web applications using no-code tools, focusing on logic, user experience, and digital problem-solving rather than syntax.

Digital Marketing Essentials

An introduction to online marketing fundamentals including content creation, social media strategy, and digital branding — tailored for young learners.

Exploring Arduino

Students explore electronics and physical computing, learning how software interacts with hardware through sensors and components.

Music Production (BandLab)

Students explore digital music creation, sound design, and basic production techniques using industry-friendly tools.

Online Streaming & Content Creation

An introduction to streaming fundamentals, digital presence, and responsible content creation in a structured, age-appropriate environment.

Smart Tools

An interactive workshop introducing students to AI, machine learning, and smart tools through hands-on projects using platforms like ChatGPT and Scratch.

UI/UX & Web Design Fundamentals

An introduction to user interface and user experience design, helping students understand how digital products are designed for real users.

Building a Chatbot

Students design and build simple chatbots while learning the basics of conversational logic, automation, and AI-driven interaction.

Format: In-person or live online
